Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Master Chef Abdul Rahman, Bhai Biryani started as a small street-side stall in the old city of Hyderabad. Today, we're a beloved restaurant known for preserving the authentic taste of Hyderabadi biryani.

Our secret lies in the traditional dum cooking method, where marinated meat and aromatic basmati rice are slow-cooked in sealed handis, allowing the flavors to infuse perfectly.
